FAQ

Short answers to common questions.

How is the Deal Grade calculated?

Weighted score from ROI (5y), cash flow, net yield and gross yield, tuned to Dubai buy‑to‑let benchmarks.

What assumptions does Basic use?

Defaults: 3% annual appreciation, 0% rent growth, +2% expenses/yr. Pro adds full growth scenarios.

Can I export a client‑ready PDF?

Yes. Use “Download Professional Report” to generate a multi‑page PDF with summary and breakdown.

Is my data saved?

No account needed for Basic; data stays in your browser during the session.
