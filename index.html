Grade & Rent guidance
Grade iInvestment Grade is a weighted score (0–100) tailored to Dubai buy‑to‑let: ROI (5y) 25%, Net Yield 25%, DSCR 20%, IRR (5y) 15%, Gross Yield 10%, Cash Flow 5%. Targets: ROI ≥60% (5y), Net ≥6%, DSCR ≥1.20x, IRR ≥12% (5y), Gross ≥8%, Cash Flow ≥ AED 0/mo. Each metric contributes linearly up to its target (capped), then summed to 100. Letter bands: A ≥85, B 70–84, C 50–69, D 40–49, F <40.

Example
ROI 50% → 50/60 ≈ 0.83 × 25 ≈ 21
Net 5.0% → 5/6 ≈ 0.83 × 25 ≈ 21
DSCR 1.10× → 1.10/1.20 ≈ 0.92 × 20 ≈ 18
IRR 10% → 10/12 ≈ 0.83 × 15 ≈ 12
Gross 7.0% → 7/8 ≈ 0.88 × 10 ≈ 9
Cash flow AED 1,000 → 1000/2000 = 0.50 × 5 = 3
Total ≈ 84 → Grade B+
