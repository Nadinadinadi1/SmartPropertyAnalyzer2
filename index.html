    What drives your grade

      Core Metrics

      — DSCR iDebt service coverage ratio. Measures ability to pay the mortgage from operations: NOI ÷ monthly P&I. 1.0 = break‑even; higher is safer. Healthy ≥ 1.20x; borderline 1.00–1.20x.
      — Net Yield iAnnual net operating income after operating costs and vacancy ÷ purchase price. Shows asset efficiency, independent of financing. Healthy ≥ 6%; fair 4–6%.
      — Gross Yield iAnnual rent ÷ price before any expenses. Quick market comparator; pair with Net Yield for realism. Healthy ≥ 8%; fair 6–8%.
      — Cash Flow iMonthly liquidity: effective rent + other income minus operating costs and mortgage. Negative means you must fund the shortfall. Healthy ≥ AED 0/month; buffer ≥ AED 500.
      — ROI (5y) iTotal return over five years from cash flow, principal paydown, and assumed appreciation. A simplified estimate for quick comparison. Healthy ≥ 60% (5y); fair 40–60%.
