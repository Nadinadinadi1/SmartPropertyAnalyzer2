Why this works

We focus on what moves decisions: realistic rent and price, financing costs, and operating expenses. The analyzer keeps assumptions explicit and tuned to Dubai, then shows how each change affects cash flow, yields and DSCR. Too often, terms like “ROI” are vague or computed in ways that look good but don’t hold up. This analyzer is built to cut through that and grade a deal on real performance — fast, transparent, and easy to explain to clients and lenders.

Methodology

We follow the same straightforward approach used by banks and professional investors. Start with rent, subtract the real costs of running the property to get income after expenses, and compare that to the mortgage payment. That shows cash flow today and resilience in different financing conditions. The analyzer uses sensible Dubai defaults so you can move fast, and every assumption can be adjusted in the steps above. The result is simple, defensible math — no tricks, just a clear view of performance.

Who it’s for

Brokers and buy‑to‑let investors who need a quick, consistent, and defensible view of a deal before deeper diligence.
